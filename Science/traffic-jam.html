Phantom
Traffic Jam.

A small brake tap on a dense highway can become a backward-moving wave. This prototype turns the NJ Turnpike idea into a small laboratory: change demand, reaction time, spacing, trucks, rain, and a disturbance, then watch when smooth flow becomes unstable.
