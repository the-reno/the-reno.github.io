The jam that came from nowhere.

You crept through traffic for twenty minutes waiting for the accident. The road opened up and there was nothing there. The jam was the accident. One nervous driver over-braked. The car behind reacted harder. A wave propagated backward at 15 km/h while every car moved forward. The person who started it was long gone.
