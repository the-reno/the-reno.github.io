The jam that came from nowhere.

You have been in traffic that stops for no reason. You creep forward for twenty minutes, waiting for the accident, the roadwork, the reason. Then the road opens up and there is nothing there. The jam was the reason. One driver braked a little too hard. The car behind reacted. The one behind that reacted harder. By the time the wave had traveled a mile backward, the person who started it was long gone.

This was formalized in the 1990s and observed on real highways. The wave is stable and persistent.
