Why traffic suddenly stops

You are moving, the road looks clear, and then everything slows down for no obvious reason. This model shows how a tiny brake tap can ripple through traffic and grow into a stop-and-go wave. No crash. No lane closure. Just reaction, spacing, and pressure building over time.
