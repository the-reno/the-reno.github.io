The jam that came from nowhere.

You waited twenty minutes for the accident. The road opened up and there was nothing there. One driver braked harder than necessary. The car behind reacted harder still. A wave traveled backward at ~15 km/h while every car moved forward at 90. The person who started it was long gone by the time you reached the slow zone.

model: Intelligent Driver Model (Treiber, Hennecke & Helbing 2000) · validated on real circular-track experiments (Sugiyama 2008)
