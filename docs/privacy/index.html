Privacy Policy

Effective July 18, 2026

Ronu is a static personal website. It does not ask you to create an account, submit personal information, or provide payment information.

Information collected

The site does not use first-party analytics, advertising pixels, cookies, browser storage, or tracking scripts. Interactive demonstrations run in your browser, and their inputs are not transmitted to the site owner.

Hosting and server logs

The site is hosted by GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information such as your IP address, browser details, requested pages, and timestamps to deliver and secure the service, subject to GitHub's own privacy terms.

External resources

Some pages load fonts from Google Fonts. The dice demonstration also loads KaTeX from cdnjs. When those resources load, the respective provider may receive technical request information such as your IP address and browser details.

Data sharing and retention

The site owner does not receive or retain personal data through this static website and therefore does not sell or share visitor information. Infrastructure and external-resource providers may retain technical logs under their own policies.

Children's privacy

The site is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the website's data practices or service providers change. The effective date above identifies the current version.
